Software Contest Rules & Regulations

Eligibility Criteria

  • Only students from CSE, IT, and AIDS departments are eligible to participate.
  • Students must be currently enrolled in the college.
  • Valid college ID card is mandatory for participation.
  • Students must register with their correct roll number and register number.

Registration Process

  • Registration is mandatory for participation.
  • Each student can register only once.
  • All information provided during registration must be accurate.
  • Registration details cannot be modified after submission.

Contest Guidelines

  • Participants must arrive 30 minutes before the contest start time.
  • Laptops must be fully charged and in working condition.
  • Internet access will be provided during the contest.
  • Use of mobile phones is strictly prohibited during the contest.

Important Notes

  • Participants must carry their college ID card on the day of the contest.
  • Any form of malpractice will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
  • College reserves the right to modify rules at any time.
